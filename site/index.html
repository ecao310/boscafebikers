Your first ride

Never ridden with a group before? This is the one to start with.

  • No-drop, always. We regroup at every light, bridge and hill. Nobody gets left behind.
  • All levels welcome. Most rides are 10–20 flat-ish miles at a conversational pace.
  • BlueBikes are perfect. Plenty of us ride them. Any bike that rolls is the right bike.
  • Bring a lock, a water bottle, and enough cash or card for a coffee.
  • Helmets encouraged, lights required if we're out near sunset.

About us

Boston Café Bikers is a casual cycling group built around the two best things about this city: the riding and the coffee. We meet up somewhere reachable by T or bike, roll out together, and finish at a café worth the detour — Somerville, Brookline, the Charles paths, the Minuteman, and wherever else looks good that week.

There's no membership, no dues, and no drop. Just show up, say hi, and order something warm at the end.
